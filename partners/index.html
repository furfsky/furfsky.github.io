Partner Program

The Staff team of this server is always interested in growth opportunities, and as such we have opened our doors to partnerships with other servers and communities. We see partnerships as a way to grow both of our communities and connect in a way that might not have been possible otherwise.

Partnership Requirements:
- 2,500 or more members
- Hypixel Skyblock related community
- Non-toxic, non-ToS breaking community

If you don't meet the member requirements but still want to work with us, we will be glad to partner, but you will get a few less perks than most partners. Speaking of partnership perks, here is what we have to offer:

Partnership Perks:
- Server invite and description posted in #partners in the Discord server
- Any of your server announcements in #partner-announcements
- Access to our #partner-chat
- Staff members on your Discord get free access to FurfSky Reborn #alphas
- Moderation support if needed
